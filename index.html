ABOUT ME

My name is DUSABIMANA Arthur Junior and I am a Web Application Developer, currently living in Rwanda, Kigali. I have a Bachelor of Science in Computer and software engineering from the University of Rwanda, and my primary focus and inspiration for my studies is Web Development. I am both driven and self-motivated, and I am constantly experimenting with new technologies and techniques.
